Our Story

Making tourism accessible for everyone

At Open Routes, we believe every person deserves the opportunity to explore El Salvador. Our mission is to reduce barriers and share travel information that helps visitors move with confidence.

We combine destination research, accessibility tools and community insight so more people can enjoy beaches, towns, lakes and mountains without feeling left out.

Mission

Promote accessible tourism through inclusive routes and reliable travel information.

Vision

Become a leading platform for accessible tourism in El Salvador.

Values

Inclusion, trust, independence and respect guide every route we share.
